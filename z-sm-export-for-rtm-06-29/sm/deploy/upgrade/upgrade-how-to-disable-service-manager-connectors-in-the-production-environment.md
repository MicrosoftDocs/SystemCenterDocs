# How to Disable Service Manager Connectors in the Production Environment
Use the following procedure to disable the [!INCLUDE[smshort](../../../sm/deploy/deploy-guide/includes/smshort_md.md)] connectors in the production environment.  
  
### To disable a connector  
  
1.  In the Service Manager console, click **Administration**.  
  
2.  In the **Administration** pane, expand **Administration**, and then click **Connectors**.  
  
3.  In the **Connectors** pane, select the connector that you want to disable.  
  
4.  In the **Tasks** pane, under the connector name, click **Disable**.  
  
5.  In the **Disable Connector** dialog box, click **OK**.
