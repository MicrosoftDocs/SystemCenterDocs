  <introduction>
    <para>Information Technology (IT) departments must manage changes to their IT environment and the risk associated with such changes. The change management features in <token>smlong12</token> help you manage change by providing repeatable, predictable, and measured processes to implement change.</para>
    <para>The topics in this section are organized according to common change management scenarios. Even though the sample scenarios refer to a fictitious organization, Woodgrove Bank, the scenarios and steps are based on real use, and they describe how to use the change and activity management features in <token>smshort</token>.</para>
  </introduction>
