# How to Remove an Activity from a Workflow
Use this procedure to remove an activity from a workflow in the [!INCLUDE[smatfull2012](../../../sm/manage/author/includes/smatfull2012_md.md)]. This operation does not remove the activity from the Activity Library or from the **Activities Toolbox** pane.  
  
### To remove an activity from a workflow  
  
-   In the authoring pane, right\-click the activity, and then click **Delete**.
