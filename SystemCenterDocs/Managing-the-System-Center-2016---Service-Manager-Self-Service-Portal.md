# Managing the System Center 2016 - Service Manager Self-Service Portal
The [!INCLUDE[smssp](Token/smssp_md.md)] in [!INCLUDE[smlong12](Token/smlong12_md.md)] is used by end users to contact help desk personnel for help requests. Users can also search the knowledge base, perform tasks, and manage their requests.
